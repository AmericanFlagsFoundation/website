Mental health affects every one of us. At the American Flags Foundation, we believe that breaking the stigma around mental health starts with open, honest conversations.

## The Silent Struggle

Every year, millions of Americans struggle silently with anxiety, depression, PTSD, and other mental health challenges. The fear of judgment keeps too many people from seeking the help they deserve.

According to the National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI), 1 in 5 U.S. adults experience mental illness each year — yet fewer than half receive treatment. The gap between suffering and support is enormous, and stigma is the biggest barrier.

## Why We Exist

That is why the American Flags Foundation was created. Our mission is clear:

- **Shatter the silence** surrounding mental health
- **Embrace empathy** for those who are struggling
- **Build hope** through community support and resources
- **Break barriers** to accessing mental health care
- **Foster resilience** in individuals and communities
- **Cultivate optimism** for a future free of stigma

## You Are Not Alone

If you or someone you know is struggling, remember — reaching out is not weakness. It is courage.

**Crisis Resources:**
- **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline** — Call or text 988, available 24/7
- **Crisis Text Line** — Text HOME to 741741
- **SAMHSA National Helpline** — 1-800-662-4357
- **Veterans Crisis Line** — 988, then press 1

Together, we can build a world where mental health is treated with the same urgency and compassion as physical health.

*The American Flags Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to combatting mental health stigma and challenges.*
